📋 Installation Instructions

  1. Click "Download All Favicons" button above or download individual sizes
  2. Save all PNG files to your public/ folder
  3. The favicon.svg is already in your public folder
  4. Refresh your browser and clear cache (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
  5. Your new favicon should now be visible!
